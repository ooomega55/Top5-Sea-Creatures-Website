Seal

Information About Seals:

  • "Earless" seals lack an external ear flap, present on fur seals and sea lions, but they still have ears present beneath the surface of the skin.
  • Mothers are more responsive to the calls of their own pup than to non-filial pups after only three days. Mother seals' responses are also varied.
  • Research on hooded seals illustrated a drop in brain temperature by 3 degrees Celsius over the course of a fifteen minute dive.
  • Because seals are typically found along coastlines, they primarily consume fish, squid, and shrimp, as well as other crustaceans, mollusks, and zooplankton organisms.
  • While humans acquire it from burning tobacco, researchers think seals' blood carbon monoxide levels are connected to their deep dives.
