Turtles

Information About Turtles:

  • Many turtles are able to retract their heads and feet into their shells while sea turtles have lost the ability to retract their heads.

  • Most turtles are active during the day, spending their time foraging for food.

  • Sea turtles travel from the ocean to lay eggs on beaches. Usually, sea turtles lay around 110 eggs in a nest, though the flatback turtle only lays 50 at a time.

  • The largest sea turtle species is the leatherback turtle. It weighs 600 to 1,500 lbs.

  • The shell is made up of about 60 bones that are covered by plates called scutes.
